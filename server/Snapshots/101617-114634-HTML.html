Employee Data Privacy Notice

By joining this application, you are accepting that Flex share your personal information such as you home address to Flex transportation vendor and you accept the Flex Employee Data Privacy Notice

Flextronics International Limited and its affiliates ("Flex") takes its obligations to protect Employee Personal Data very seriously. Flex collects, holds and processes personal information about current and former employees, applicants and contractors ("Employee Personal Data") and commits to comply with all applicable privacy and data protection laws in relation to it. Flex has implemented regular data privacy training in relation to data privacy laws. Employees must comply with the Data Privacy Standards, the Global Privacy Policy Statement and the Manual for Processing Personal Data. You can access a copy of these and other policies and procedures on the Data Privacy Portal or by contacting HR Global Business Services.

Employee Personal Data

Flex collects Employee Personal Data by telephone, email and various other sources including:

  • Personal details such as name, date of birth, home address, national tax ID, social security number, driver's licence number, passport number and personal email address;
  • Family details such as marital status, name of partner, children's details and mother's name;
  • Education and training details such as qualifications, academic records, schools, training record and professional expertise
  • Employment details such as employment status, job role, hire date, work location, termination date, status, appraisal details and organisational details such as company employed by, office address, work phone number, individual photo, department and supervisors, cost centre, employee type and whether full time or part time, work email address, intranet user log in, supervisor details, HR adviser details, other email addresses, job description, codes and employee ID;
  • Financial details such as benefit details, stock ownership, pay, expenses, pay cheque information, bank account information, bonus targets and pensions information;
  • Racial and ethnic origin for equal opportunities management subject to applicable local law;
  • Trade union membership;
  • Religious beliefs or other beliefs of a similar nature;
  • Physical or mental health or condition; and
  • Information on criminal offences and sentences.
  • Purposes of processing

Flex will process Employee Personal Data in order to operate its business and administer the employment relationship.

The purposes for which Flextronics processes your Employee Personal Data may also include:

  • Payroll administration;
  • Administration of employee compensation and benefits arrangements and employee care services;
  • Stock administration;
  • Recruitment and headcount management;
  • Administration of personnel records (for example, in relation to leave, absences, pay and benefits);
  • Business travel;
  • Training;
  • Career and professional development and talent management;
  • Performance management and appraisals;
  • Maintaining directories and facilitating and recording communications;
  • Safety and support information for workers’ compensation claims;
  • Contact with next of kin;
  • Disciplinary and grievance proceedings;
  • Authorisation controls and data security;
  • Backup and business continuity;
  • To verify compliance with Flextronics' policies and procedures and detection of inappropriate behaviour including prevention of fraud or theft and risk management;
  • Compliance with contractual, legal and regulatory obligations and dealing with legal claims and disputes;
  • Protecting intellectual property, confidential information and our assets; and
  • Management forecasts and planning changes in group structure.

Disclosures of Employee Personal Data

  • Flex may disclose Employee Personal Data to Flex group companies or third parties where the disclosure is for specific legitimate business or as required by law or regulation including to:
  • Payroll administrators;
  • Suppliers and contractors who support Flextronics operations including who administer personnel records, employee benefits, recruitment or training;
  • Other suppliers and customers;
  • Employment and recruitment agencies;
  • Other members of the Flextronics Group including staff in HR, IT, business managers and HR Global Business Services;
  • Intermediaries and business partners;
  • Professional advisers such as accountants and lawyers;
  • Government and law enforcement bodies as required by law; and
  • Potential acquirers or purchasers in relation to disposals of any of Flextronics' business or assets.

All Flex staff must handle Employee Personal Data carefully and only use and disclose it as required in accordance with their duties and treat it as confidential at all times.
